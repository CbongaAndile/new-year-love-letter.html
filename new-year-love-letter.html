Mamarh, as we welcome the first day of 2026, ngifuna wazi ukuthi how deeply loved you are and how ukufika kwakho in my life has been nothing but a divine blessing. Every moment with you feels like a beautiful dream I never want to wake up from. As you are reading this entire love letter, try to visualize me ngiphambi kwakho, ngikubambe ngesandla and ngikutshela konke lokhu okubhalwe lana.

Our story began on November 14, 2025 at 21:36, when I first saw you on Facebook. We weren't even friends then, but I was so captivated by your beauty that I had to comment on your story. I told you how beautiful you are and how unfair it was that you didn't have a diamond ring on your finger. Little did I know that comment would change my life forever.

We immediately connected and talked every day. The connection was instant and profound. By November 22, 2025, I could no longer hide my feelings for you. I told you I love you, and to my eternal joy, you told me you love me too (even though I noticed ukuthi you don't like being vulnerable but ngiyabonga nje ukuthi you did express yourself ngendlela owazi ngayo). We were both healing from the past and agreed we weren't ready for a relationship, but our hearts had other plans.

Then fate intervened beautifully. I discovered your father lives nearby, and you were coming to visit him. We decided to meet. On December 1, 2025 around 16:00, we went on our first date at Burger King. You were so breathtakingly beautiful, stunning, gorgeous—every wonderful word in the dictionary couldn't fully capture your radiance. I still remember your smile after our first kiss—that magical moment when you're kissing your partner and you both feel each other smiling during the kiss. We paused, looked at each other, smiled, and continued kissing. Even before that kiss, from the moment we met and throughout our Bolt ride to Burger King, you were smiling, happy, and everything I'd ever dreamed of. I still remember how big my smile was the night when I got home from us hanging out for the first time.

The next day, December 2, 2025. That was quality time spent in the most beautiful way. It's only been about 48 days since we met, but I've never been happier in my entire life.

The fact that you accepted me and loved me despite both our toxic and traumatic relationship experiences is one of the many reasons I want to spend the rest of my life with you. There is nothing I want more than spending each and every second with you and I am trying to be the best partner you could ever wish for. You've shown me what true love means—healing, accepting, and growing together.

Happy New Year, Sthandwa Sami. May 2026 be the beginning of forever for us.
